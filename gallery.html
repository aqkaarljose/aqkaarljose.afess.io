Share the knowledge

We conduct career symposiums and seminars for aspiring aviators to pursue careers in aviation. This will help them decide what is the best career for them when they grow up.

Tools room

The tools are all clean and well-maintained, and they are clearly arranged in a way that makes the mechanics easy to find.

Flight Simulator

Flight simulator setup with three monitors on a desk. The monitors are arranged in a wide arc, which would provide the pilots with a panoramic view of the virtual world. The center monitor is directly in front of the user, and the two side monitors are angled outwards. This setup would allow the user to see the sides of the airplane and the horizon more realistically.

Practice makes perfect

We value the importance of primacy and ensure that you are taught the right thing starting from day 1, giving you the skill and confidence to pilot your own aircraft. At AFES, every flight hour counts towards the perfection of your flying skills, because we believe that practice does make one perfect.

Maintenance

Our maintenance team is second to none in the country, ensuring the safety of the crew on each and every flight. Aircrafts also undergo periodic maintenance check upon reaching 50 and 100 hours for this reason it is vital to always measure the flight time of aircrafts.

Earn your wings

Earn your wings through the guidance and supervision of our talented team of instructors. We know that each student is unique, and our methods adjust to suit each individual. We will take you through the various stages/ratings of flight training, including: student pilot license, private pilot license and commercial pilot license, as well as the instrument rating.
